Methodology/assumptions

It is important that these calculations are accurate, so I encourage criticism. Please let me know on the Junior Doctors' Facebook group, or email me at info@busily.co.uk.

Counting time

Definitions (NHS Employers contract offer, page 6):

Shift Type Times Pay rate
Plain Time 7am-10pm Mon-Fri
7am-7pm Sat 		Base-rate
Saturday Time 7pm-10pm Sat Time-and-a-third
Sunday Time 7am-10pm Sun Time-and-a-third
Night Time 10pm-7am Mon-Sun Time-and-a-half

The only thing to be aware of is a case such as a shift starting on Saturday at 8pm, finishing Sunday 8am, would count as:

  • Total Hours: 12
  • Plain Hours: 0
  • Night Hours: 9 (i.e. 10pm Saturday to 7am Sunday)
  • Saturday Hours: 2 (i.e. 8pm Saturday to 10pm Saturday)
  • Sunday Hours: 1 (i.e. 7am Sunday to 8am Sunday)

NHS Employers' rotas calculate this incorrectly: e.g. Rota 6 appears to count the final 3 hours of week 5 Saturday's night shift as "Plain Time".

Proposed Package calculations

Basic Pay

Less-Than-Full-Time employees Basic Pay will be reduced by the percentage they work, e.g. LTFT F5 (20-23.9hrs/wk) would be reduced by half. See for example the RCoA LTFT A-Z guide (page 6).

London Weighting

Assuming this is unchanged, as it isn't reference by NHS Employers.

Assuming Less-Than-Full-Time employees receive the percentage of this weighting that they work (e.g. LTFT F5 = 50%)

North Central and East London, West London, South London attract a £2,162 weighting, as set out in the NHS Pay and Conditions Circular (M&D) 1/2015 (page 14).

(Resident staff receive a lower weighting of £602, but this calculator does not allow for this)

Additional Rostered Hours

"Additional rostered hours are any hours per week above the 40 basic, full-time hours. They will be paid at the basic hourly rate, with pay enhancements for hours falling in unsocial hour periods." (NHS Employers contract offer, page 8)

Additional Rostered Hours = Total Hours - 40, up to a maximum of 8.

Hourly Rate is Basic Pay / 52 / 40 (i.e. 52 weeks a year, 40 hours a week)

(Note that Less-Than-Full-Time employees Basic Pay has already been reduced, so is not calculated again)

Annual Additional Rostered Pay = Average Weekly Additional Rostered Hours * Hourly Rate * 52

Sunday Hours

Sunday Weighting = 33%

Annual Sunday Pay = Average Weekly Sunday Hours * (Hourly Rate / 3) * 52

Saturday Hours

Saturday Weighting = 33%

Annual Saturday Pay = Average Weekly Saturday Hours * (Hourly Rate / 3) * 52

Night Hours

Night Weighting = 50%

Annual Night Pay = Average Weekly Night Hours * (Hourly Rate / 2) * 52

On-Call Allowance

The percentage you provide is taken of the Basic Pay.

(Note that Less-Than-Full-Time employees Basic Pay has already been reduced, so is not calculated again)

Programme Premia

The Current Grade and Specialty you provide are used to calculate this (see NHS Employers contract offer, page 23):

  • General practice (in vocational training practice placements): £8,200 p.a.
  • Emergency Medicine (ST4 and above): £1,500 p.a.
  • Psychiatry (ST1 and above): £1,500 p.a.

Assuming Less-Than-Full-Time employees receive the percentage of this premia that they work (e.g. LTFT F5 = 50%)
